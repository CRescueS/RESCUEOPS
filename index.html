Field Medical

MARCH PROTOCOL REFERENCE

M - MASSIVE HEMORRHAGE
• Apply Tourniquet (High & Tight)
• Pack junctional wounds with hemostatic gauze
• Apply pressure dressings
A - AIRWAY
• Head-tilt/Chin-lift or Jaw-thrust
• Clear obstructions/suction
• Consider OPA/NPA if unconscious
R - RESPIRATIONS
• Seal open chest wounds (occlusive dressing)
• Monitor for tension pneumothorax
• Assist ventilations if < 10 or > 30 bpm
C - CIRCULATION
• Check radial pulses
• Re-assess tourniquets
• Prevent hypovolemic shock (keep warm)
H - HYPOTHERMIA / HEAD
• Replace wet clothing
• Use space blankets / heat pads
• GCS / Pupil assessment
